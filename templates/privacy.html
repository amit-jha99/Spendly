Privacy Policy

Last updated: 31 May 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Spendly (the "Service") collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the Service, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Data We Collect

We collect only the information needed to provide the Service:

  • Account information — such as your name, email address, and password, provided when you register.
  • Expense data — the transactions you log, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.
  • Usage information — basic technical details such as your browser type and the pages you visit, used to keep the Service running reliably.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the expense tracking features.
  • Display your spending summaries, categories, and trends back to you.
  • Secure your account and protect against unauthorised access.
  • Respond to your questions and support requests.

We do not sell your personal or financial data, and we do not use it for advertising.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and is associated with your account. We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time, after which we will remove it within a reasonable period, except where retention is required by law. We recommend keeping your own backups of any records important to you.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on a small number of third party services — for example, hosting providers or fonts loaded from a content delivery network — to operate. These providers process data only as necessary to perform their function and are not permitted to use your personal data for their own purposes. We do not share your expense records with advertisers or data brokers.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through your account settings. We will do our best to address your concerns promptly.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above.
